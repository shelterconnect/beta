Welcome to ShelterConnect

ShelterConnect is a free and open-source platform that connects homeless shelters with restaurants and volunteer organizations.

How It Works

Shelters

Shelters distribute food. Food gets where it needs to go, into the mouths of the hungry and underserved.

Restaurants

Restaurants contribute surplus food for collection and contribution to the movement.

Volunteer Group

Neighbors, groups, organizations, and churches unite to improve Los Angeles via volunteerism at various events.
